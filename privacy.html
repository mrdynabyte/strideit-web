Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data Stride It collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Overview

Stride It ("the App") is developed and operated as an independent application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use Stride It on your iPhone and Apple Watch. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

Stride It collects the minimum data necessary to provide its core functionality. Specifically:

  • Step count data — read from Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission. This includes daily step totals recorded by your Apple Watch.
  • App preferences — settings such as your daily step goal, preferred language, and notification preferences. These are stored locally on your device.
  • Device information — basic information such as device model and operating system version, used solely for app compatibility and crash diagnostics.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly for support.

3. HealthKit Data

Stride It integrates with Apple HealthKit to read your step count. We request read-only access to step data. We do not write data to HealthKit, and we do not share HealthKit data with third parties, advertisers, or any external services.

HealthKit data is processed entirely on your device or within your personal iCloud account. It is never transmitted to our servers.

4. How We Use Your Data

The data collected by Stride It is used exclusively to:

  • Display your daily, weekly, and monthly step progress.
  • Calculate your progress toward your personal step goal.
  • Track your level and achievements within the gamification system.
  • Send daily notification reminders with your remaining step count.
  • Display widget content on your iPhone home screen and lock screen.
  • Show complications on your Apple Watch face.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data or health data with any third parties. Stride It does not use advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or tracking frameworks of any kind.

6. Data Storage & Security

All data processed by Stride It remains on your device or in your personal iCloud account, governed by Apple's own privacy and security practices. We do not operate external servers that store your personal or health data.

App preferences may be synced via iCloud between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's standard sync mechanisms, which are protected by end-to-end encryption under Apple's iCloud privacy policy.

7. Notifications

Stride It may send you local push notifications to remind you of your daily step goal. These notifications are generated entirely on your device using your current step data — no data is sent to external servers to generate them. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications → Stride It.

8. Children's Privacy

Stride It is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

9. Your Rights

Since Stride It does not collect or store personal data on external servers, there is no account or profile to delete. All app data resides on your device and can be removed by:

  • Deleting the app from your iPhone and Apple Watch.
  • Revoking HealthKit access in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Stride It.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@strideit.app
